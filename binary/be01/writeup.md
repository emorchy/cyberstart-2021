`binwalk -Me chicken.pdf` will recursively extract all files

Layout:
chicken.pdf
└── egg.zip
    └── chicken.zip
        └── egg.zip
            └── chicken.zip
                └── egg.pdf
chicken.pdf -> egg.zip -> chicken.zip -> egg.zip -> chicken.zip -> egg.pdf
